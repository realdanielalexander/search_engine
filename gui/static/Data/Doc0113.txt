<TITLE>FLUOR &lt;FLR> UNIT GETS CONSTRUCTION CONTRACT</TITLE>
<BODY>Fluor Corp said its Fluor Daniel
unit received a contract from Union Carbide Corp &lt;UK> covering
design, procurement and construction of a 108 megawatt combined
cycle cogeneration facility in Seadrift, Texas.
    The value of the contract was not disclosed.
